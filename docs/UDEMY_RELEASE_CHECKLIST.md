# Udemy Publication Checklist

## Course landing page

- [ ] Final course title and subtitle approved.
- [ ] Course description clearly states audience, outcomes and practical value.
- [ ] At least four specific learner outcomes are entered.
- [ ] Requirements and prerequisites are accurate.
- [ ] Intended learners are defined.
- [ ] Instructor profile, biography and image are current.
- [ ] Course image and branding are licensed and readable at thumbnail size.
- [ ] Promo video is concise, accurate and representative of the course.
- [ ] Search terms are relevant and not misleading.

## Curriculum

- [ ] Sections and lectures follow the approved eight-module sequence.
- [ ] Lecture titles are action-oriented and clear.
- [ ] Total runtime is between 2 and 4 hours unless change approval exists.
- [ ] Every lecture has a verified video or approved article/resource.
- [ ] Preview lectures are selected.
- [ ] Activities, quizzes and practical exercises are correctly placed.
- [ ] Workbook and downloadable tools open successfully.
- [ ] No source files, answer keys or confidential working material are exposed.

## Media

- [ ] Video exports play from start to finish.
- [ ] Audio is intelligible and consistent.
- [ ] Slides and screen demonstrations are readable.
- [ ] Captions are uploaded and manually proofed.
- [ ] Transcripts match the final edit.
- [ ] File names and versions are controlled.
- [ ] No notifications, personal data, credentials or third-party copyrighted content are visible.

## Learner experience

- [ ] Welcome message is approved.
- [ ] Completion message directs learners to the next action.
- [ ] Course navigation and expectations are explained.
- [ ] Exercises contain clear instructions and expected outputs.
- [ ] Support boundaries and response expectations are stated.
- [ ] A mechanism exists for learner feedback and defect reporting.

## Commercial configuration

- [ ] List price and market positioning approved.
- [ ] Launch coupon strategy approved.
- [ ] Affiliate and promotional settings reviewed.
- [ ] Tax, payout and instructor account information verified.
- [ ] Revenue reporting owner and cadence confirmed.
- [ ] Launch date and campaign dependencies confirmed.

## Submission gate

The course owner records:

- release-candidate version;
- QA report links;
- unresolved accepted risks;
- final runtime and lecture count;
- actual production hours and initial investment;
- submission date;
- Udemy review feedback and remediation issue links;
- public course URL and publication date.

Submission is blocked by any open P0 or P1 defect.
